Claude overtakes OpenAI in enterprise

Anthropic hit 34.4% enterprise adoption vs. OpenAI's 32.3%, a reversal from under 8% a year ago, driven by Claude Code authoring an estimated 4% of all public GitHub commits.

Three AI security incidents in seven days

Confirmed in one week: an AI-assisted Apple M5 kernel exploit, the first criminal AI zero-day targeting open-source infrastructure, and OpenAI devices compromised in an NPM supply chain attack.

Washington and the states move on AI regulation

Trump signals an executive order on AI safety; Colorado and Connecticut pass major AI laws in the same week, with the EU extending high-risk AI compliance deadlines. Three jurisdictions shifted simultaneously.

xAI deprecates eight Grok models overnight

Under SpaceX ownership, xAI retired eight API models simultaneously with automatic redirects and no migration window. Enterprise teams with active xAI integrations face an immediate audit item.

Anthropic launches vertical AI for legal and SMB

Claude for Legal integrates with Thomson Reuters, LexisNexis, and DocuSign. Claude for Small Business ties into QuickBooks, HubSpot, and Google Workspace. Freshfields reported 500% usage growth in six weeks.

Gates Foundation and Anthropic commit $200M over four years

The partnership deploys Claude in global health systems, K-12 education across three continents, and agricultural productivity tools for smallholder farmers — a four-year operational program, not a pilot.
