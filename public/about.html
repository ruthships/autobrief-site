About AutoBrief

Stay updated with the latest in AI.

Stay updated with the latest without drowning in the feed.

Keeping up with AI is a 24/7 job. Models ship overnight, regulation moves fast, and the headlines never stop. Yet your clients and your team still need clear, decision-ready context. AutoBrief is how we keep pace: an AI-generated weekly podcast that distills the stories that matter to the C-suite into a briefing you can finish between meetings.

How it's built

Every week, AutoBrief runs an automated pipeline from raw headlines to finished episode, newsletter, and website, with AI handling the heavy lifting at each stage.

Research Agents

Built by Sandra Djuffo Djouda and Louis-Charles Genereux

We built AI agents that scan the sources that matter: industry news, policy shifts, funding rounds, and enterprise moves. They rank and synthesize what belongs in the room where decisions get made.

AI Voice Generation

Built by Tina S Zhang

Each script is shaped in the rhythm of the podcasts we admire most, then voiced with ElevenLabs for a consistent, natural delivery that turns a week's headlines into audio worth listening to.

AI-Generated Assets

Built by Ruth Tupe

Every episode triggers a Claude skill that produces two deliverables: a designed HTML email newsletter for the inbox, and an update to this website with the same headlines, summaries, and story breakdowns. One pipeline, two places to read or listen.
